# Privacy Policy for RankedSleep

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

**Contact:** support@rankedsleep.app

## Introduction

RankedSleep ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. RankedSleep is a sleep-tracking and wellness app that helps you understand, improve, and compare your sleep health.

## Information We Collect

### Apple Sign-In Data

- **Apple ID**: Used for secure authentication via "Sign in with Apple"
- **Email Address**: Optional; only if you choose to share it with us through Apple Sign-In
- **User Identifier**: Anonymous Apple user token for authentication
- **Usage**: Authentication data is used solely to identify your account and secure your data

### Health Data from Apple Health

- **Sleep Data**: Sleep duration, bedtime, wake time, sleep stages (deep, REM, core)
- **Heart Data**: Heart Rate Variability (HRV), Resting Heart Rate (RHR)
- **Respiratory Data**: Respiratory rate, blood oxygen saturation (SpOâ‚‚)
- **Usage**: Health data is used to calculate your daily Sleep Score and track sleep patterns
- **Storage**: Health data is synced to our secure backend for score calculation and historical tracking

### User-Generated Content

- **Username**: Optional display name for social features
- **Profile Information**: Basic profile data you choose to share
- **Sleep Scores**: Daily Sleep Score calculated from your health data
- **Friend Connections**: Your friend list and social connections within the app

### Anonymous Usage Data

- **App Performance**: Crash reports and performance metrics (anonymous)
- **Feature Usage**: Which app features you use most (no personal health details)
- **Interaction Patterns**: General app navigation trends to improve user experience

## What We DON'T Collect

- **Raw Health Data Sharing**: Your raw health metrics (HRV, RHR, SpOâ‚‚, etc.) are NEVER shared with friends or other users
- **Location Data**: We don't track or use your location
- **Third-Party Tracking**: No advertising trackers or analytics that identify you personally
- **Browsing History**: No tracking of activities outside the app

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

- **Sleep Score Calculation**: Analyze your health data to compute a personalized daily Sleep Score (0-100)
- **Sleep Insights**: Provide actionable insights about your sleep quality, consistency, and patterns
- **Sleep Debt Tracking**: Calculate rolling sleep debt to help you understand recovery needs
- **Trend Analysis**: Show historical trends and progress over time (7-day, 30-day, 90-day views)
- **Social Features**: Enable friendly competition through leaderboards and friend comparisons

### Data Processing

- **Score Computation**: Health data is processed to calculate sleep scores using our proprietary algorithm
- **Baseline Calculation**: Rolling 14-day baselines for HRV and RHR (requires 7+ valid nights)
- **Historical Storage**: Your sleep scores and summaries are stored for trend analysis
- **Anonymous Analytics**: Usage patterns analyzed anonymously to improve the app

### Social Features Privacy

- **Friend Visibility**: Friends can only see your Sleep Score and username
- **Health Data Protection**: Raw health metrics remain completely private
- **Opt-Out**: You can control your leaderboard visibility in Settings
- **Friend Requests**: All social connections require mutual approval
